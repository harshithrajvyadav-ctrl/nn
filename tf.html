Our Story

At Sweet Crumbs Bakery, we believe every bite should tell a story. Founded in 2015 by master baker Maria Gonzalez, our bakery has been delighting customers with handcrafted pastries, artisan breads, and custom cakes. We use only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods to create treats that warm the heart and satisfy the soul.
